### POST 6 - DAY 6

💰 Let's Talk About Cost: Senior Care Budget Guide

One of the most common questions: "How much does senior care cost?"

Here's a quick breakdown of average monthly costs:

🏘️ Independent Living: $2,500-$4,500
🏥 Assisted Living: $4,500-$6,500
🧠 Memory Care: $5,500-$8,000
⚕️ Skilled Nursing: $8,000-$12,000+
🏠 Home Care: $25-50/hour
💚 Hospice Care: Usually covered by Medicare

Important to know:
✓ Costs vary by location
✓ Financial assistance may be available
✓ Long-term care insurance may help

Want help finding options that fit your budget? Our assessment considers your financial situation.
